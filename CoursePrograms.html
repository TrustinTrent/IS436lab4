Undergraduate Programs

Undergraduate:

Explore the dynamic intersection of technology and innovation with our comprehensive undergraduate courses in Information Systems at the University of Maryland, Baltimore County (UMBC). Our curriculum is designed to provide students with a solid foundation in information technology, data management, and systems analysis.

Courses offered include:

  • IS436 Structured Systems Analysis and Design
  • IS 420 Database Application Development
  • IS 425 Decision Support Systems
  • IS 451 Network Design and Management

Graduate:

Elevate your expertise in Information Systems with our advanced graduate courses at the University of Maryland, Baltimore County (UMBC). Our graduate program is designed for aspiring professionals and researchers seeking an in-depth understanding of cutting-edge technologies and strategic applications.

To get more specific, and personalized, information about graduate level Information Systems classes at UMBC, please contact us with an intent to register.

Here are some of the programs that you might be interested in:

  • MS in Information Systems – On Campus
  • MS in Information Systems – Online
  • MS in Human-Centered Computing (HCC)
  • MPS in Health IT
  • MPS in Software Engineering
  • Or a number of different online certifications including (SEC+, NET+)
